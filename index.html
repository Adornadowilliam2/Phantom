Uvogin

Illumi Zoldyck

Temporary Member#4 of Phantom Troupe

Illumi Zoldyck is not an official member of the Phantom Troupe but temporarily joins them during a specific arc in the series. He is a highly skilled assassin and a member of the infamous Zoldyck family. Illumi possesses the ability to manipulate needles and control people's actions through his "Needlemen" technique. Although he is not a permanent member, Illumi aids the Phantom Troupe in their mission during his time with them.

Kortopi

Member#12 of Phantom Troupe

Kortopi is another member of the Phantom Troupe who possesses the ability to conjure objects. He can create copies of any non-living object he has touched before. Kortopi's ability, called "Gallery Fake," allows him to replicate items perfectly, making him a valuable asset to the group. He joined the Phantom Troupe after being recruited by Chrollo.
